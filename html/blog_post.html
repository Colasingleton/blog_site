A little more about me...

What drives me to become a software engineer Is a desire to be a part of something creative.

I love building things and the process of discovery. When something isn’t in my current wheelhouse of possibilities, I go searching and inquiring to learn all that I can. I appreciate the flexibility to build something that doesn’t have a rule book.

Creatively, I function best with little supervision and a world of swatches.

I study hard so that I can be a part of a team of great engineers, building something the world will love.
